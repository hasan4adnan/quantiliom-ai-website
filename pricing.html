Frequently
asked questions.

Can't find what you're looking for?
Reach out to our team.

Can I switch plans anytime?
Yes — upgrade or downgrade at any time. Upgrades take effect immediately and are prorated to the day. Downgrades apply at the end of your current billing cycle, so you keep full access until then.
Is there a free trial for Pro?
Yes. Every Pro signup includes a 14-day free trial with no credit card required. You get full Pro access during the trial. If you choose not to continue, you'll automatically move to the Starter (free) plan with your data intact.
What happens when my trial ends?
You'll automatically move to the Starter plan at no charge. All your project data and architecture outputs are preserved — you just lose access to Pro-only features like PDF export and cost reports. Upgrading again restores everything instantly.
How does annual billing work?
Annual plans are billed as a single upfront payment — Pro at $276/year (saving $72), Team at $756/year (saving $192). You can switch from monthly to annual at any time; remaining monthly balance is applied proportionally to your annual invoice.
Can I add more than 5 team members?
The Team plan supports up to 5 members. For larger teams, our Enterprise plan offers unlimited seats with custom pricing. Contact us and we'll get you a quote tailored to your team's size and needs — typically set up within one business day.
Do you offer student or nonprofit discounts?
Yes — we offer 50% off Pro and Team plans for students with a valid .edu email address, and significant discounts for registered nonprofits. Reach out to our team with your credentials and we'll get you set up within 24 hours.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex, Discover), PayPal, and bank wire transfer for annual Enterprise contracts. All payments are processed securely via Stripe with full PCI compliance.
