Cost
Intelligence

Infrastructure costs are invisible until they become a crisis. Cost Intelligence makes every monetary consequence of your architecture visible before you write a line of code. It breaks down estimated monthly infrastructure spend by component, detects expensive choices (Firebase at scale, over-provisioned compute, redundant managed services), and suggests concrete cheaper alternatives with estimated savings.

Key Capabilities
  • Monthly infrastructure cost breakdown by component (compute, DB, cache, storage, bandwidth)
  • Expensive choice detection — flags Firebase, over-provisioned EC2, over-engineered microservices at early stage
  • Concrete cheaper alternatives with estimated percentage savings
  • Low / medium / high estimates based on traffic scale assumptions
  • Third-party service cost modelling (Stripe fees, SendGrid, Twilio)
